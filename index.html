Frequently asked questions

What inspired you to pursue a career in computer science?

I have always been interested in technology and the way it can solve problems. When I was in high school, I took a programming course and became fascinated by the power of code to create innovative solutions. This inspired me to pursue a career in computer science and become a part of the ever-evolving technological landscape.

What are your qualifications and experience in computer science?

I have a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Mbarara University of Science and Technology, where I gained knowledge in programming, computer systems, algorithms, and data structures.I also hold a high school certificate from HALCYON HIGH SCHOOL. I have experience working on several projects, including a self-balancing robot that utilizes PID control to maintain its balance. I have also volunteered to teach data structures and algorithms, and introduction to programming at the university.

How do you keep up with industry trends and technologies?

I stay up-to-date with industry trends and technologies by attending conferences and workshops, reading industry publications, participating in online communities, and continuously learning new skills. I believe that keeping up with the latest developments in the field is crucial to providing the best possible service to my clients.

What types of projects have you worked on in the past?

During my studies, I have completed several projects, including a self-balancing robot that uses PID control to maintain its balance. I have also worked on developing algorithms for detecting and tracking objects using computer vision, as well as building web applications using Python and Django.

How do you approach working on a project?

I begin by meeting with the client to determine their goals and needs. I then create a project plan that outlines the scope, timeline, and resources required. Throughout the development process, I provide regular updates and feedback to ensure that the project is meeting the client's expectations. I am also open to feedback and willing to make adjustments as necessary to achieve the desired outcome.

What are your areas of expertise?

My areas of expertise include robotics, programming, data structures and algorithms, web development, computer vision, and machine learning. I have gained experience in these areas through my education, volunteer work, and personal projects..
